Voice Controlled Maze

If the ball is red, that means voice control is not connected. This means that the ball can only be controlled using the WASD keys. Try putting a link to your model in the box at the top and hitting submit. When the ball turns blue, voice control will turn on, and it is your job to use voice commands to guide the ball to the green square at the end. Finish the maze or reload the page for a completely new one. Enjoy!
